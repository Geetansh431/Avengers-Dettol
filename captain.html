Captain

Origin Story: Steve Rogers, a frail young man, volunteers for a top-secret experiment during World War II and is transformed into the super-soldier Captain America.

World War II: Captain America fights against the Nazis and their allies as a symbol of American courage and heroism. He battles alongside the Howling Commandos and becomes a propaganda tool for the Allies.

Avengers

Frozen in Ice: Towards the end of the war, Captain America is presumed dead after a battle with the villainous Red Skull. He is later discovered frozen in ice and revived decades later in modern times.

Avengers: Captain America becomes a founding member of the superhero team known as the Avengers. He plays a crucial role in leading and organizing the team to combat various threats to Earth.

Civil War

Civil War: Captain America clashes with Iron Man and the government over the Superhuman Registration Act, leading to the Marvel Civil War storyline. He becomes a fugitive fighting for individual liberty against government oversight.

Death and Rebirth: Captain America is assassinated by Crossbones, with the manipulation of the Red Skull, leading to a period where his former sidekick Bucky Barnes takes up the mantle. However, Steve Rogers is later resurrected and reinstated as Captain America.

EndGame

Infinity War and Endgame: Captain America fights alongside the Avengers against cosmic threats like Thanos, culminating in the epic battles of "Avengers: Infinity War" and "Avengers: Endgame." In the latter, he wields Mjolnir and plays a pivotal role in defeating Thanos and restoring the universe.

Retirement: After the events of "Endgame," Captain America chooses to retire from active duty, passing on his shield to Sam Wilson (Falcon), who becomes the new Captain America. Steve Rogers lives out his life with Peggy Carter in the past, appearing as an old man in the present timeline.
